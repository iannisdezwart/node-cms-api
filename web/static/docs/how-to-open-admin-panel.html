Home > How to open the NodeCMS admin panel

Summary

To open the NodeCMS admin panel, enter the domain name of your website (e.g. example.com) into the URL bar of your browser and append "/admin-panel" to it, without the quotes. Then, press the enter key on your keyboard.

(In case your domain name is example.com, you will have to write example.com/admin-panel in the URL bar.)

After you pressed enter, you will see this window:

Log in with your username and password.
Once you are logged in, you can click on the tabs on the left (or bottom if you visit the admin panel on mobile) to manage your website.
You will find additional documentation and guides on how to operate the admin panel here.
