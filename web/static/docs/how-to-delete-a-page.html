Home > Page Management > How to delete a page

Summary

In the "Pages" tab you can delete pages. If you delete a page, it will be destroyed and will not appear anymore for the users of your website. You cannot undo this action.

Example

To delete a certain page,

  1. open NodeCMS admin panel
  2. Click on the "Pages" tab
  3. Click on the "delete" button of the certain page

Not all page types have a "delete" button. Only page types that are add-able do. See the example below:

Clicking on "delete" in the above example will delete the page "My third page". It will be removed from the list.

When you clicked on "delete", you will get a popup. If you are sure you want to delete the page, click "Delete Page". To cancel, click on the X on the top right of the popup.
Warning: you cannot undo the deletion of a page!

The page will be destroyed and you will get a confirmation popup message.
The page will be removed from the its list.
