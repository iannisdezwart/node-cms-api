Summary

The "Pages" tab is the place where you can manage the pages of your website. It is used to add, edit or delete pages of different kinds.

To open the page manager,

  1. open NodeCMS admin panel
  2. Click on the "Pages" tab as visualised below
  3. You might be promted to enter your password. In this case, fill in your password.

Then, you will see something like this:

1 (red): The page manager view

2 (dark blue): Two page type, there could be more, or less, depending on your website. Each page type is seperated by a small blank space

3 (green): Some page types can have sub pages. In this case, "Text_page" has two of them
You can add, remove and edit sub pages as mentioned on the buttons
You can swap the order around using the arrows
